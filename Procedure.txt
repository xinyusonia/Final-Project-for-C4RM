We get some data related inflation risk. Data including Gasoline Inflation, 
Food inflation, HealthCare Inflation, College Inflation, Airfare Inflation,
Unemployment Rate and Federal Funds Effective Rate. We used 5 years of monthly data and put it in a cdv file. Below are our data references:

https://www.usinflationcalculator.com/inflation/
U.S. overall Inflation and energy, food & health care inflation

https://data.bls.gov/timeseries/LNS14000000
U.S. bureau of labor statistics

https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/FEDFUNDS
U.S. Federal funds effective rate
